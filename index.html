Welcome to Swift COD Form

Quick Setup Guide

Getting started takes less than 2 minutes. Follow these simple steps to activate the app on your store.

1 Customize Your Form

Open the Swift COD Form app in your Shopify admin. Navigate to the COD Forms tab. Use the live preview canvas to change your button colors, form text, and required fields. Click Save when you are happy with the design.

2 Add the Button to Your Store

  1. From your Shopify Admin, go to Online Store > Themes.
  2. Click Customize on your live theme.
  3. Navigate to your Default Product Page using the top dropdown menu.
  4. On the left sidebar, click Add Block under the Product Information section.
  5. Select Swift COD Button and drag it below the Buy Now button.
  6. Click Save.

3 Manage Your Orders

When a customer places an order via the popup, it will instantly appear in two places: Your native Shopify Orders tab (as an unfulfilled order), and the Swift COD Orders Dashboard, where you can easily verify, cancel, or add tracking numbers in bulk.

Advanced Features

Setting Up Shipping Rates

Want to charge for delivery? Go to the COD Forms tab and click Manage Shipping Rates. Add a rate (e.g., "Standard Shipping - Rs. 200"). You can even set conditional rules to make shipping free if the order value is over a certain amount!

Connecting Your Pixels

Go to the Settings tab inside the app. Paste your Meta (Facebook), TikTok, or Google Pixel IDs into the respective boxes. Click Save. The app will now automatically track conversions with zero code required.
