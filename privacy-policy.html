Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy will help you better understand how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

This privacy notice sets out how Swift COD Form ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and discloses any personal information that you give us or that we collect when you use our Shopify app.

  • Store Information: Your shop domain and basic account details required to authenticate the app securely.
  • Order Information: Information related to Cash-on-Delivery orders placed through our form (customer names, shipping addresses, phone numbers) strictly for fulfilling the order.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We use the collected information solely to provide and operate the Swift COD Form app interface, process and sync Cash-on-Delivery orders directly into your Shopify admin panel, and provide technical support.

3. Data Retention & Security

We do not store your customers' personal data on our own servers longer than is technically necessary to process the immediate transaction. All order data is routed directly to your secure Shopify database. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

4. Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Reading this privacy notice will help you understand your privacy rights and choices. If you still have any questions or concerns, please contact us at support@yourdomain.com.
