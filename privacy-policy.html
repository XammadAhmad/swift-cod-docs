Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Swift COD Form ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you install or use our app in connection with your Shopify-supported store.

1. Information We Collect

When you install the App, we automatically access certain types of information from your Shopify account:

  • Store Information: Your shop domain and basic account details required to authenticate the app securely.
  • Order Information: Information related to Cash-on-Delivery orders placed through our form, including customer names, shipping addresses, and phone numbers, strictly for the purpose of syncing the order to your store.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and operate the Swift COD Form app interface.
  • Process and sync Cash-on-Delivery orders directly into your Shopify admin panel.
  • Provide technical and customer support.

3. Data Retention & Security

We do not store your customers' personal data on our own servers longer than is technically necessary to process the immediate transaction. All order data is routed directly to your secure Shopify database. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
