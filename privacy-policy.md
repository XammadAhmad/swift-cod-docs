Swift COD Form ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you install or use our app in connection with your Shopify-supported store.

## Information We Collect
When you install the App, we automatically access certain types of information from your Shopify account:
* **Store Information:** Your shop domain and basic account details required to authenticate the app.
* **Order Information:** Information related to Cash-on-Delivery orders placed through our form, including customer names, shipping addresses, and phone numbers, strictly for the purpose of fulfilling the order within your store.

## How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information solely to:
* Provide and operate the Swift COD Form app.
* Process and sync Cash-on-Delivery orders to your Shopify admin.
* Provide technical support.

## Data Retention
We do not store your customers' personal data on our own servers longer than necessary to process the immediate transaction. All order data is routed directly to your secure Shopify database.
